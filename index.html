About Me

Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for design and aesthetics and a commitment to communication using technology. I studied both design and psychology in college, following up on my interest in technology by earning certification in Web Development. I also value interpersonal relations and teamwork, both of which I nurtured at Children’s National Medical Center where, as a Child Psychiatric Specialist, I assessed and responded to patient needs. I have experience in React, Bootstrap and jQuery, and see myself working as part of a team in the field of Web Development where I can continue to respond to the changing demands of the field as a lifelong innovator.
